Honey-Soy Glazed Salmon
This dish is quick, flavorful, and pairs perfectly with steamed rice or roasted vegetables.
Prep time: 5 minutes
Cook time: 10 minutes
Servings: 2
Ingredients
2 Salmon fillets (6 oz each)
3 tbsp Soy Sauce (Allergen: Soy)
2 tbsp Honey
1 tbsp Fresh ginger, minced
1 clove Garlic, minced
1 tbsp Olive oil
Optional: Green onions or sesame seeds for garnish
Instructions
Whisk the Glaze: In a small bowl, combine the soy sauce, honey, ginger, and garlic.
Sear the Salmon: Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-high heat. Place salmon skin-side up (if skinless) or flesh-side down and cook for 3–4 minutes until golden brown.
Flip and Glaze: Flip the fillets. Pour the honey-soy mixture into the pan.
Reduce: Let the sauce bubble and thicken for 2–3 minutes while the salmon finishes cooking. Spoon the glaze over the fish as it thickens.
Serve: Remove from heat once the salmon flakes easily with a fork. Garnish with green onions if desired.
